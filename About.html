Jo Jo’s Events started with one simple thing: a passion for making events personal. From organising family celebrations to taking charge of every detail, our founder quickly became the go-to person for creating events for family and friends.
Before long, that passion turned into helping others plan some of life’s biggest moments — including weddings. Between timelines and table plans, ” Joanne's experience was growing. To take things to the next level, our founder completed a floristry course, adding a creative floral touch to every celebration.
Today, Jo Jo’s Events brings together organisation, creativity, and bounds of heart to create events that feel effortless, personal, and unforgettable.
Ours is to turn your event into an unforgettable experience. We are here to curate, plan and lead your day with confidence - creating an unforgettable day.
