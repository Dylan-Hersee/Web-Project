Jo Jo’s Events started with one simple thing: a serious love for planning parties. From organising family celebrations to taking charge of every detail, our founder quickly became the go-to person for making events look amazing and run smoothly.
Before long, that passion turned into helping others plan some of life’s biggest moments — including weddings. Between timelines, table plans, and “don’t forget the flowers,” Jo Jo’s Events was growing. To take things to the next level, our founder completed a floristry course, adding a creative floral touch to every celebration.
Today, Jo Jo’s Events brings together organisation, creativity, and a whole lot of heart to create events that feel effortless, personal, and unforgettable.
Our mission is simple: to turn your ideas into unforgettable experiences. We’re here to take the stress off your shoulders and replace it with excitement, creativity, and confidence.
At Jo Jo’s Events, we believe every celebration should feel personal, beautifully styled, and thoughtfully planned — whether it’s an intimate gathering or a once-in-a-lifetime wedding. We handle the details, so you can focus on enjoying every moment.
